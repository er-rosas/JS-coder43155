About us

According to many of our customers, we were pioneers in making Gaming accessible to everyone. In a time when a Gaming PC used to be synonymous with unaffordable components, we led the way in assembling high-performance systems at affordable prices and offering top-notch peripherals at reasonable rates.

TechXperience was the first company to support a Uruguayan e-sports team and accompany them to the pinnacle of success. We were thrilled by PEX's achievements and take pride in having been a part of what was undoubtedly a legendary team in our country's e-sports scene.

We also served as the primary sponsor for S2N, the most prominent local team with the most remarkable achievements in recent times.
